The Atelier

Every stitch tells a story of devotion

LittleOnes was born in 2018 when our founders — both parents — could not find products that were truly luxurious and truly sustainable. So they built it. Today every product is made in certified fair-trade facilities, using only materials we would put on our own children.

01
Organic Sourcing
Every fiber traced to its origin. GOTS & OEKO-TEX certified throughout.
02
Fair Trade
Artisans paid fairly. Communities supported. No exceptions, ever.
03
Zero Waste
Packaging from recycled ocean plastic and seed paper only.
04
1% for the Planet
One percent of all revenue funds global reforestation projects.
